Transformational Leadership,

Building on Bass's (1985) insights, the focus is on motivating followers through an engaging vision, intellectual encouragement, and personalized attention. In our charity event simulation, my team and I mistakenly categorized our efforts as transformational because we thought we were making a notable impact. However, we were actually practicing Transactional Leadership: we defined specific tasks, offered rewards for their completion, and prioritized performance metrics. This error highlighted my lack of understanding of the essential traits of transformational leaders, particularly the skill to craft a vision that extends beyond immediate objectives and to promote intrinsic motivation in team members. The power of Transformational Leadership is in its ability to resonate with followers' values and inspire innovation, yet critics caution that it can veer into manipulation if the leaders vision is primarily self-serving and lacks ethical grounding (Tourish, 2013).
